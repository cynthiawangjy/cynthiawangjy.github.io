Website Redesign

Redesigning and developing a local bakery's website to make it modern and responsive

Period 2024
Role UI/UX Designer & Developer

Problem

Edelweiss Bakery needed a modern, responsive website to better showcase their products and improve customer experience. The existing site was outdated and not mobile-friendly.

Solution

I redesigned and developed a completely new website with modern UI/UX principles, ensuring it was fully responsive and optimized for all devices.

Key Features

  • Responsive design for all devices
  • Modern, clean aesthetic
  • Improved navigation and user experience
  • Optimized for performance

Results

The new website successfully modernized Edelweiss Bakery's online presence, providing customers with a better browsing experience and improved accessibility across all devices.

Learnings

This project reinforced the importance of responsive design and user-centered approach when working with local businesses. Understanding the client's needs and translating them into effective digital solutions was key to the project's success.
