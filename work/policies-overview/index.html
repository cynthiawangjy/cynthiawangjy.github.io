Introduction

Immuta — Policies Overview Dashboard

Helping businesses visualize and manage their data policies

During my internship at Immuta, I was initially tasked with improving how users monitored policy synchronization — the process that enforces data access rules across connected sources.

However, what started as a small visibility fix revealed a much larger gap: users lacked a clear understanding of how their policies behaved across the platform. I expanded the project into a 0→1 Policies Overview Dashboard, designed to give organizations real-time insight into policy health, synchronization progress, and impact — bringing greater transparency and control to Immuta’s data governance ecosystem.

TIMELINE 6 weeks (within 12-week internship) July – August 2024
ROLE UX Design Intern Data Visualization, Visual Design, Product Thinking
FOR Businesses to monitor their data in real time
