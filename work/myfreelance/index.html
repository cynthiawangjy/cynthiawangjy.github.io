MyFreelance

Building a platform for freelancers to manage their business

This project focused on creating a comprehensive platform for freelancers to manage their business operations. The existing tools were fragmented and didn't provide a unified solution for project management, client communication, and financial tracking.

I led the design of an integrated platform that combines project management, client communication, invoicing, and time tracking into a single, cohesive experience.

TIMELINE 6 weeks
ROLE Product Designer
FOR Personal Project
WITH Solo project

The Problem

Fragmented tools

Freelancers were using multiple disconnected tools for different aspects of their business, leading to inefficiency and data silos.

Complex workflows

Managing projects, clients, and finances required switching between different platforms, creating unnecessary complexity and potential for errors.

Our Solution

Unified Platform

Created a single platform that integrates all essential freelancer tools - project management, client communication, invoicing, and time tracking - into one cohesive experience.

Key Features

Project Management

Comprehensive project tracking with timelines, milestones, and task management to keep freelancers organized and on track.

Client Communication

Built-in messaging and collaboration tools to streamline communication with clients and maintain project transparency.

Financial Tracking

Integrated invoicing, expense tracking, and financial reporting to help freelancers manage their business finances effectively.

Key Learnings

Integration is key

Users value seamless integration over individual feature depth. A well-connected system is more valuable than isolated powerful tools.

Workflow matters

Understanding the actual workflow of freelancers was crucial for designing an effective solution that fits their real needs.

Simplicity wins

Even complex functionality needs to feel simple and intuitive. The goal was to make freelancing easier, not more complicated.
