Hi! How was your day?

My background in visual arts, particularly drawing and painting, began when I started taking art classes at six years old. I discovered UI/UX towards the end of high school but didn't officially begin my product design journey until late 2023 with CozyLink. I quickly fell in love with the field for its ability to merge creativity and practicality, transforming ideas into tangible products that people can actually use and interact with.

In my everyday life, I’m obsessed with anything colorful, cute, and fluffy. Outside of designing, I love crocheting Miffys, cooking tomato-egg noodles, watching historical Chinese dramas, and decorating my room with Riso prints and blind box figurines.
