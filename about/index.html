I'm Cynthia 汪嘉怡

creator & learner, listener & storyteller, pixel-pusher & product-thinker

I'm a product designer recently graduated from Brown University with a Bachelor's in Visual Art while studying at the Rhode Island School of Design. I've designed products from consumer apps to enterprise platforms, working across both large companies and scrappy startups.

At the heart of my work is a care for the emotional impact of design — how it can empower people, spark joy, and make technology feel a little warmer. Whether I'm pushing pixels in Figma or matching closing tags in VS Code, I'm always thinking about how design can connect with people on a deeper level.

When I'm not designing, you'll probably find me obsessing over anything colorful, cute, or fluffy. (Current obsession: Chiikawa!)
