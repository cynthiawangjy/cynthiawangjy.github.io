Hey there! I'm Cynthia.

I’m a designer who loves sparking delight through my designs and creating more human experiences. I love listening to stories in order to better understand people so I can effectively tackle their pain points.

I’m currently pursuing a B.A. in Visual Art at Brown University and concurrently taking courses at the Rhode Island School of Design. I’m originally from San Diego, CA and am currently based in Providence, RI.

I come from a visual arts background and first heard about UI/UX design towards my senior year of high school. Although it’s always been in the back of my mind, I didn’t officially start product design until last year with CozyLink and have mostly worked on web design projects. I immediately fell in love with product design because of its user-centric and practical approach to problem-solving.

Outside of designing, I’m a huge enjoyer of anything colorful, cute, and fluffy. I enjoy crocheting and knitting, cooking and baking, watching K-Dramas and C-Dramas, and collecting Riso prints from local art fairs to decorate my room.
